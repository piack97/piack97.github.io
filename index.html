About Me

I am an astronomical instrumentalist focused on building the next generation of spectrographs to enable science in the era of massive surveys. My research focuses on the optical design, construction, and commissioning of novel multi-object spectrographs in the optical to near-infrared wavelength regime.

As the Matt Johns Instrumentation Fellow at Carnegie Observatories, I work on cutting-edge astronomical instrumentation projects that push the boundaries of our observational capabilities.
