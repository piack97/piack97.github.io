About Me

I am an instrumentalist passionate about building the next generation of astronomical spectrographs to enable science in the era of massive surveys. My research focuses on the optical design, construction, and commissioning of multi-object spectrographs in the optical to near-infrared wavelength regime.

Research

  • Discovery and characterization of exoplanets
  • Variable stars and stellar lifecycles
  • Galaxy formation and cosmology

Selected Publications

  • “New Worlds: Detecting Earth-like Exoplanets” – Astrophysical Journal, 2024
  • “Variable Stars in the Andromeda Galaxy” – Monthly Notices of the RAS, 2023
  • “Outreach and Education in Modern Astronomy” – Science Communication, 2022
