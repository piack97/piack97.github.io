I am an astronomical instrumentalist passionate about building the next generation of spectrographs to enable science in the era of massive surveys. My research focuses on the optical design, construction, and commissioning of novel multi-object spectrographs in the optical to near-infrared wavelength regime. I spend most of my time working on The Via Project, a set of twin fiber-based multi-object spectrographs designed to study the interstellar medium and dark matter around the Milky Way.

Project Roles

  • Deputy Instrument PI for The Via Project
  • Instrument Scientist for FALCON
  • Optical Designer for the GMT commisioning camera
